Sick of skimming serienjunkies.org for new releases every day?

SJgrabber assists you to grab links from the latest episodes for your favourite TV shows. Search () through all recent releases with SJgrabber. It grabs releases via RSS and their links (e.g. uploaded.to, share-online.biz, ...) by parsing HTML pages from serienjunkies.org.

Organize your favourites & copy links to your clipboard!

Star your queries () and automatically grab links of all matched releases. Click on any filehoster button to copy the link to your clipboard (get jDownloader for a perfect grabbing experience).

Clean UI, setup once and forget!

No f**king ads and no adware included! First (search) then (star) your queries, that's it. Happy grabbing!
