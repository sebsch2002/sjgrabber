Sick of skimming SJ for new releases every day?

SJgrabber assists you to grab links from the latest episodes for your favourite TV shows. It grabs releases via RSS and their links (e.g. uploaded.to, share-online.biz, ...) by parsing HTML pages from serienjunkies.org.
